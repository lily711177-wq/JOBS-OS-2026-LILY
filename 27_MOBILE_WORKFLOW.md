# MOBILE & OFFLINE WORKFLOW
## For Days You're Away from Your Computer

---

### CORE TRUTH
You can execute Phases 1-3 from your phone.
Phases 4-7 need a computer.

---

### MOBILE EXECUTION (Phases 1-3)

**Phase 1 (Fetch):**
- Check LinkedIn Saved Search emails on phone
- Check Google Alert emails
- Forward any good leads to yourself

**Phase 2 (Filter):**
- Open each JD link on phone
- Quick pass: $120K+? Vancouver/Remote? TEER 0/1? No coding?
- Save good ones to a "Jobs to Apply" note

**Phase 3 (Score):**
- For each saved job, ask: "Can I do this? Have I done something like this?"
- Gut-check score: High/Medium/Low

**Save for desktop:** Note which roles need full alchemy.

---

### WHEN YOU'RE BACK AT DESK

Execute Phases 4-7 for the roles you identified on mobile.

**Time needed:** ~2 hours for 3 roles.

---

### MAXIMUM OFFLINE

If no internet:
- Review Master Corpus (01) — memorize your achievement modules
- Practice interview answers (05) out loud
- Review negotiation scripts (06)
- Plan your LinkedIn content (24)
- Read company websites / values for target companies (save offline)

---

### SYNC PROTOCOL

If switching devices:
1. Save TRACKING.md changes
2. Save any new job links
3. Email yourself the tracking update
4. Resume on new device
