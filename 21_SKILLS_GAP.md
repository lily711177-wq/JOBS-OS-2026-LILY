# SKILLS GAP ANALYSIS
## For Each Priority Role — Identify + Close Gaps Fast

---

### HOW TO USE
For each JD, extract the requirements and score yourself:
- **Match:** You have this skill and can prove it
- **Close:** You have adjacent experience that can be framed
- **Gap:** You don't have this — decide: learn, reframe, or skip?

### CLOSING STRATEGIES BY GAP TYPE

**For "Close" gaps (adjacent experience):**
> Don't learn it. Frame your existing experience as equivalent.
> 
> *"I haven't worked in telecom, but I've led operations in a highly regulated B2B environment, which is the same complexity set."*

**For "Gap but learnable" (certification, tool):**
> Invest 2-10 hours to get the basics.
> 
> HubSpot RevOps certification: 2 hours, free
> SQL basics: 3-5 hours on LinkedIn Learning
> Change management cert: 1 week, paid (~$500)

**For "Gap and not worth it" (deep expertise):**
> Skip the role or reframe the gap as intentional:
> *"I don't have that specific skill, but I bring fresh perspective from having built systems in a different context — and I've never met a problem I couldn't figure out."*
