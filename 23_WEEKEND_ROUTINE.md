# WEEKEND & OFF-HOURS ROUTINE
## Deeper Work While the System Runs

---

### SATURDAY (Deep Work Day)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00 - 10:00 | Company deep research: 5 target companies, read their values, news, leadership |
| 10:00 - 11:00 | Skill building: close 1 gap from 21_SKILLS_GAP.md |
| 11:00 - 12:00 | LinkedIn content: write 1 post for the week ahead |
| 12:00 - 13:00 | Career page scan: check all 12 bookmarked companies for new roles |
| 13:00 onwards | Personal time — no job search after 1 PM Saturday |

---

### SUNDAY (Rest + Prep)

**Morning:**
- Week review: What worked? What didn't?
- Plan Monday's 3 targets
- Prep any documents needed for the week

**Afternoon:**
- **NO JOB SEARCH ACTIVITY**
- Rest, recreation, relationships
- You cannot be "on" 7 days a week. Sunday is non-negotiable.

---

### EVENING ROUTINE (Weekdays)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 8:00 PM | Close all job search tabs |
| 8:00 - 8:15 PM | Quick brain dump: any ideas for tomorrow? Write them down |
| 8:15 onwards | Personal time — no screens if possible |
| 10:30 PM | Sleep (gives you 7.5 hours before 6 AM wake) |

---

### THE NIGHT BEFORE (Prep for Morning)

- [ ] Check job alerts for anything posted overnight
- [ ] Identify 3 target roles for tomorrow if not already planned
- [ ] Lay out any documents you'll need
- [ ] Set alarm for 5:50 AM (10 min buffer before 6 AM start)
- [ ] Phone on Do Not Disturb overnight
