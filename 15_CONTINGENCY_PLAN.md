# CONTINGENCY & ESCALATION PLAN
## What Happens at T+30, T+60, T+90

---

### BEFORE T+30 (Weeks 1-4): NORMAL OPERATIONS

**Expectation:**
- 40-60 applications sent
- 5-10% response rate (2-6 conversations)
- 0-2 first-round interviews
- 0 offers

**Actions:**
- Execute daily workflow as designed
- Track everything
- Don't panic — this is normal

**Decision gate at T+30:**
- [ ] At least 40 applications sent?
- [ ] At least 3 conversations started?
- [ ] At least 1 interview booked?
- [ ] Response rate >5%?

**ALL YES → Continue as planned.**
**MOSTLY YES → Slight course correction (refine resume, adjust targeting).**
**MOSTLY NO → Escalate to T+30 contingency.**

---

### T+30 CONTINGENCY (No Offers, Low Response)

**Strategy Shift: From Cold → Warm**

| Change | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Increase network reach | 5 coffee meetings/week instead of 3 |
| Activate executive recruiters | Reach out to 10 Vancouver recruiters on LinkedIn |
| Reduce daily apps | 3→2/day, use saved time for higher-quality applications |
| Expand title range | Add "VP Operations," "Director of Business Operations," "Head of Strategy" |
| Expand geography | Include Toronto, Calgary, Remote Canada (wider net) |
| Post LinkedIn content | 2x/week thought leadership |

**New additions:**
- Target companies where you have 2nd-degree connections
- Attend 1 industry event per week
- Ask 1 person/week for referral

**Re-evaluate at T+45.**

---

### T+60 CONTINGENCY (No Offers, Still Low Response)

**Strategy Shift: Expand Scope**

| Change | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Lower title floor | Add "Manager" roles if $120K+ (TEER 0/1 still applies) |
| Expand geography | Include all of Canada remote, plus consider US if visa possible |
| Consider contract work | Fractional COO, interim operations, consulting gigs |
| Agency route | Apply to operations consulting firms (Deloitte, Accenture, McKinsey) |
| Industry pivot | If SaaS isn't responding, try different industries aggressively |
| Resume overhaul | Full rewrite — test entirely different positioning |

**Re-evaluate at T+75.**

---

### T+90 EMERGENCY PROTOCOL

**If no offers at 90 days:**

1. **Take a survival role** — any operations/management job that pays $80K+. It's not failure. It's buying time.
2. **Continue applications** at reduced cadence (5/week instead of 15/week)
3. **Re-evaluate strategy** honestly:
   - Is the resume working? Get professional reviews.
   - Is the targeting wrong? Broader scope.
   - Is the market bad? Float above the market until it recovers.
4. **PR filing is still on track** — don't let job search derail PR
5. **Consider startup route** — your sweet spot. Target early-stage companies that need a COO.

**Mental health:**
- If you're considering giving up, take 1 full week off completely
- Talk to someone (friend, family, therapist)
- Remember: 8 years ago you had nothing. You built $17M. That doesn't disappear.
