Just deliberate preparation for the right build.

**What I actually do:**

• **Organizational Scaling** — Take organizations from "everyone reports to the founder" to structured operations with role clarity, training pipelines, and escalation paths. Built a team from 3 to 70 with 95%+ retention.

• **Cross-Functional Leadership** — Primary interface between executive leadership and 5 operational unit managers across 32 locations. Translated strategic direction into coordinated execution plans. Reduced decision cycle time by 60%.

• **M&A / Transition Management** — Directed 8-workstream due diligence through $17M acquisition. Consolidated 8 systems into 1. Retained 100% of key team through earnout. Kept operations running through ownership change without missing a beat.

**The industry was healthcare services. But the skills are industry-agnostic.** Every scaling organization needs operational infrastructure. Every multi-location business needs coordination mechanisms. Every company going through transition needs someone who's done it before.
