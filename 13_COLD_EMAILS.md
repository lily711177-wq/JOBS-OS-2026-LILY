# COLD EMAIL OUTREACH TEMPLATES
## The Highest-Leverage Channel — Use It

---

### RULES OF COLD EMAIL

1. **Find the email:** Hunter.io, VoilaNorbert, Apollo.io, or company email pattern
2. **Send within 2 hours of applying** (reference the application)
3. **5 sentences max** — they scan on mobile
4. **One specific value mention** — not generic flattery
5. **Subject line that says what you want**

---

### TEMPLATE 1: To Hiring Manager (Post-Application)

**Subject:** Applied for [Role] — quick context

> Hi [Name],
>
> I just applied for the [Role] position. Quick context so you know I'm not a spray-and-pray applicant:
>
> I spent 8 years scaling a B2B SaaS company from 3→120 people through a $17M exit. Built every operational system from scratch — hiring, revops, strategy, compliance.
>
> I'm interested in [Company] because [specific reason].
>
> If there's alignment, I'd welcome a brief conversation.
>
> Best,
> Aryan
> [LinkedIn URL]

### SUBJECT LINE A/B TESTING

| Approach | Subject Line |
|----------|-------------|
| Direct | "Applied for [Role]" |
| Value | "Quick thought on [Company]'s [challenge]" |
| Curiosity | "Former COO → your [Role]?" |
| Referral | "Referred by [Name] — [Role]" |
| Short | "[Role] — Aryan S." |

**Test each for 10 sends. Track open rates. Use what works.**
