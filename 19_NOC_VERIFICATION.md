# NOC / TEER VERIFICATION GUIDE
## Every Role Must Qualify for PR

---

### THE RULE
Only apply to roles classified under **NOC TEER 0 or TEER 1**.

- **TEER 0:** Management occupations (directors, executives, COOs, VPs)
- **TEER 1:** University degree-required occupations (managers, strategists, analysts)

---

### TITLE-TO-NOC MAPPING

| Your Target Title | Likely NOC | TEER | Notes |
|------------------|-----------|------|-------|
| Director of Operations | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | Senior managers — must oversee managers |
| Director of Strategy | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | Same as above |
| VP Operations | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | Executive management |
| COO | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | C-suite, clear TEER 0 |
| Chief of Staff (executive) | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | If reports to CEO/COO, oversees managers |
| Chief of Staff (admin) | NOC 13100 | TEER 2 | If primarily administrative — SKIP |
| Head of Operations | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | Director-equivalent |
| Strategy & Ops Manager | NOC 10022 | TEER 1 | If manages function, not people |
| Revenue Ops Manager | NOC 10022 | TEER 1 | Technical/specialized management |
| Senior Manager | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | If oversees other managers |
| General Manager | NOC 00015 | TEER 0 | Clear TEER 0 |

---

### VERIFICATION CHECKLIST (Per Role)

Before applying, confirm:

- [ ] Title clearly listed as Director, VP, COO, Head of, Senior Manager (or equivalent)
- [ ] Job description mentions "overseeing managers" or "leading a team of [X]" (for TEER 0)
- [ ] Reporting structure: reports to C-suite or EVP level (TEER 0 indicator)
- [ ] Budget / P&L responsibility mentioned (TEER 0 indicator)
- [ ] NOT an administrative/coordinator role (TEER 2+)
- [ ] NOT a technical/specialized individual contributor role (TEER 1 but may be tight)

---

### RED FLAGS (Auto-Reject for PR Purposes)

| Flag | Why |
|------|-----|
| "Team lead" not "manager" | Usually TEER 2 |
| "Coordinator" in title | TEER 2+ |
| No direct reports in description | May not qualify as management |
| "Administrative" duties primary | TEER 2+ |
| "Analyst" in title | Usually TEER 1 or 2, harder to justify |
| Salary < $100K | May not meet PR income thresholds |

---

### WHAT TO DO IF UNCERTAIN

If a role is borderline:
1. Check the full NOC description on IRCC website:
   - https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/eligibility/find-national-occupation-code.html
2. Look for "employment requirements" section — if it requires university degree + management experience, it's TEER 0/1
3. If still unsure, apply anyway — you can decide later if the offer would qualify
