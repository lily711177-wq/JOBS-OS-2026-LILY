# DECISION FRAMEWORK
## How to Choose Between Offers

---

### THE MATRIX

When you have multiple offers, score each on a scale of 1-5:

| Factor | Weight | Offer A | Offer B | Offer C |
|--------|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Total Compensation | 25% | /5 | /5 | /5 |
| Title & Career Growth | 20% | /5 | /5 | /5 |
| Company Stage & Trajectory | 15% | /5 | /5 | /5 |
| Culture & Team Fit | 15% | /5 | /5 | /5 |
| Commute / Location | 10% | /5 | /5 | /5 |
| PR Eligibility (NOC TEER) | 10% | /5 | /5 | /5 |
| Industry Interest | 5% | /5 | /5 | /5 |
| **Weighted Total** | **100%** | **/5** | **/5** | **/5** |

**How to calculate:** (Score × Weight) for each factor, sum all.

---

### BEYOND NUMBERS (Gut Check)

Ask yourself these questions:

1. **"Do I want to work with these people every day?"**
   - If yes, that's worth 10-20% in compensation difference
2. **"Will I learn and grow here?"**
   - A stepping-stone role can be worth a lower salary
3. **"Does this role get me closer to my long-term goal?"**
   - What does "done" look like in 3-5 years?
4. **"How do I feel when I think about accepting?"**
   - Excitement? Relief? Dread? Your gut knows before your brain does.

---

### THE RULES

1. **Never decide on the same day you receive an offer.** Ask for 48 hours minimum.
2. **Always sleep on it.** Your morning brain is different from your evening brain.
3. **Talk to someone you trust.** Share the matrix. They'll see things you miss.
4. **Consider the worst case.** If the job doesn't work out, which one has the best safety net?
5. **Compensation is important but not everything.** A job you hate at $180K is worse than a job you love at $150K.

---

### ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA (Minimum Bar)

Before accepting ANY offer, confirm:

- [ ] Salary meets minimum viable number (your walk-away)
- [ ] NOC code is TEER 0 or 1 (PR eligible)
- [ ] Employer supports PR filing (provides employment letter)
- [ ] Role is what was described in interviews (no bait-and-switch)
- [ ] Commute/location is sustainable
- [ ] Gut says: "I can do this for 18+ months"
